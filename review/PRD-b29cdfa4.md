Context: **Refined Feature Request: Executive Sales & Performance Dashboard**

**Objective:**
Develop a high-fidelity executive sales dashboard for Managers to monitor organizational health through team-based comparison and longitudinal trend analysis.

**Core Requirements:**
1. **Primary Metrics (Hero Cards):**
   - **Monthly Total Revenue:** Real-time tracking of gross revenue vs. previous month targets.
   - **Sales Velocity:** Count of total sales closed within the selected period.
2. **Team Comparison Visuals:**
   - **Leaderboard Performance:** Comparative ranking of sales teams by revenue output.
   - **Longitudinal Comparison:** A multi-line chart or stacked area chart showing how teams perform relative to each other over time (Monthly/Quarterly granularity).
3. **Advanced Filtering:**
   - **Regional Slicing:** Ability to filter entire data sets by geographic regions.
   - **Time Dimension:** Toggle between Weekly, Monthly, and Year-to-Date views.

**Design Aesthetic (Ampliwork Midnight Cyan):**
- **Palette:** Deep Midnight Navy (`#020617`) backgrounds with interactive Cyan (`#00A3FF`) highlights.
- **Glassmorphism:** Use semi-transparent card backgrounds with frosted-glass blur effects.
- **UI Elements:** Use pill-shaped buttons (`rounded-full`) and strict geometric layouts.
- **Data Density:** High-information density with clear visual hierarchy, optimized for dashboard "Command Center" viewing.
