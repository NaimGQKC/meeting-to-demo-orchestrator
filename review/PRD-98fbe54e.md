## Overview

Context: **Refined Feature Request: Executive Sales & Performance Dashboard**

**Objective:**
Develop a high-fidelity executive sales dashboard for Managers to monitor organizational health through team-based side-by-side comparison and longitudinal Year-To-Date (YTD) trend analysis.

**Core Requirements:**
1. **Primary Metrics (Hero Cards):**
   - **YTD Total Revenue:** Primary financial health indicator.
   - **Sales Velocity:** Count of total sales closed YTD.
2. **Longitudinal Visualizations:**
   - **Revenue Over Time:** A primary **Line Chart** showing revenue trajectories YTD to visualize growth and momentum.
3. **Team Performance Comparison:**
   - **Side-by-Side Analysis:** Ability to compare multiple sales teams' performance concurrently on shared axes.
4. **Filtering & Slicing:**
   - **Regional Filtering:** Global filter to slice the entire dashboard by geographic region.
   - **Default View:** Dashboard must default to **Year-to-Date (YTD)** data upon loading.

**Design Aesthetic (Ampliwork Style):**
- **Palette:** Midnight Navy (`#020617`) backgrounds with interactive Cyan (`#00A3FF`) highlights.
- **Visual Style:** Glassmorphism card effects, pill-shaped UI elements (`rounded-full`), and high-contrast typography.
- **Experience:** High-density "Command Center" layout optimized for desktop and executive briefings.
